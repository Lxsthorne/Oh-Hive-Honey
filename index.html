Who We Are

We are honey aficionados with a passion for shopping local. As such, we came together to open a one-of-a-kind Southern bakery and honey goods shop! We've partnered with over a dozen family-owned beekeeping farms from around the U.S. and Canada to share their raw honey goods and the custom sweet treats we make with them.


We believe the best honey is kept local, raw, and good. That's why every honey goods purchase at Oh Hive, Honey! provides a 5% donation to the Save the Bees Foundation. Our honey is some of the best you'll ever find, but don't take our word for it.
